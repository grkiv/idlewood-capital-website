Investment Overview

Executive Summary

2710 Gervais Street represents a unique value-add opportunity in Columbia's thriving Bull Street corridor. This 15,632 square foot commercial property sits on 1.2 acres in the heart of the city's most dynamic redevelopment district.

Built in 1956 and extensively renovated in 1989 and 2004, the property has served various commercial uses and is currently configured for flexible office/retail space. The MU-1 zoning allows for diverse commercial applications, positioning the asset perfectly for the area's ongoing transformation.

The property underwent comprehensive environmental investigation in 2019, with all identified concerns successfully remediated. This positions the asset for immediate redevelopment without environmental liability concerns.

With market rents ranging from $15-35/SF depending on improvements, the property offers substantial upside potential through strategic repositioning and lease-up to capitalize on the area's strong demographic trends and limited competing inventory.
